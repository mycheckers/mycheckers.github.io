Rules of Play

The game follows classic English Draughts rules. Understanding movement and capturing is key to victory.

Movement & Capturing

  • Simple Checkers: Move diagonally forward one square.
  • Capturing: Regular pieces capture forward only. To capture, jump over an opponent's adjacent piece into an empty square behind it.
  • Combo Jumps: If you can capture another piece after landing, the move continues until no more captures are possible.
  • Choice of Path: If there are multiple ways to capture (e.g., capture one piece vs. two pieces), the player is free to choose any option.

Kings (Promotion)

  • Promotion: A simple checker becomes a King upon reaching the farthest row of the board.
  • King's Movement: Kings move diagonally both forward and backward, but only one square at a time.
  • King's Capture: Kings can capture diagonally both forward and backward. They can also perform multi-jump captures and must continue jumping as long as possible.

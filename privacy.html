Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 17, 2025

At Checkers English (accessible from mycheckers.github.io), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Checkers English and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account to play the game. However, we may collect:

  • Log Files: Standard usage data (IP address, browser type, date and time stamp).
  • Game Data: Temporary data stored in your browser (LocalStorage) to save your settings and game progress.

2. Third-Party Advertising (AdinPlay)

We use third-party advertising companies, such as AdinPlay and Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on Checkers English.

3. Cookies

Like any other website, Checkers English uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

4. Children's Information

We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. Checkers English does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13.
